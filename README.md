# VR Mirror Therapy (WebXR)

**A free, open-source Virtual Reality Mirror Therapy tool for stroke recovery and phantom limb pain.**
*Accessible directly from the browser. No installation required.*

---

## üìñ The Story Behind This Project
In 2023, my partner suffered two strokes caused by an AVM, resulting in right-side hemiparesis and aphasia. During her initial recovery, she used professional robotic/virtual systems (like 'Amadeo') with incredible results. However, after a second, more severe stroke, we lost access to that technology and were left with only a traditional physical mirror box.

Realizing the gap between expensive medical tech and what patients have at home, I decided to use my skills as a developer to bridge it. I built this tool to replicate the immersive experience of professional therapy using just a smartphone and a $10 cardboard VR headset.

I am sharing this **100% free and open-source** to help other survivors and families who cannot afford expensive equipment.

## üöÄ How to Use
1.  Open the **https://neocodebinary.github.io/vrtherapy/** on your smartphone.
2.  Select your rear camera (Wide Angle recommended).
3.  Place your phone into a Cardboard/VR headset.
4.  Put on the headset while seated.
5.  Move your **healthy limb**. The screen will flip the image, tricking your brain into seeing your affected limb move.
